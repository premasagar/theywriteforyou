They Write For You

This demonstrates the influence of British MPs in newspapers, showing those MPs who have been paid to write newspaper articles, and the potential influence of party politics in mainstream media (2008 - 2010).

Created in several hours at the Hackers & Hacks Hackday, with data from Journalisted, using ScraperWiki, Python, Ruby and JavaScript. By Jonathan, Anna and Premasagar, with Simon, Julian and Rob.

The code is available on GitHub. Feel free to download it, change it and submit it back.
