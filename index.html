They Write For You

This mashup demonstrates the influence of British MPs in newspapers, showing those MPs who have written newspaper articles, and the potential influence of party politics in mainstream media. (Rollover the newspapers, for further info).

Created in just a few hours at the data journalism "Hackers & Hacks Hackday", with data from Journalisted (from 2008-2010), using ScraperWiki, Python, Ruby and JavaScript.

By Jonathan, Anna, Prem, Simon, Julian and Rob, (with hat-tips). The code is on GitHub. Feel free to fork it, change it and submit it back.
